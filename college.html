Govt. College of Engineering and Ceramic Technology

ESTD - 1941

Established in 1941 by Professor Sasadhanr Ray who was an eminent and educationist and the first principal of the college. The college was named as Bengal Ceramic Institute Calcutta, which later on flowered into College of Ceramic Technology.

Currently I am porsuing my B.Tech in Information Technology(I.T) from here and it not an exaggeration in saying that, the teachers are not only helps us to be educated but also guide us in every path breking situation and support us through their brief knowledge of life and proffetional domain.
I feel very glad to be a part of such Organisation
Needless to say, the frequent events and activities organised by college feculty gives us a platform to embark our tallent to the world. This has become a great practice to enhance our knowledge as well as our fear over the some important era of our life.

College Events

Sky Wathing Camp

College has organised two Sky Wathing camps in two successive years i.e. 2022 and 2023 in month of January for the student to know about our neighbouring planets and to explore our vision to a next level.

Excursion to Science City

On September 20, 2022 there's a college excursion to Science City for the new commers to college but it was not restricted for 1st years only rather 2nd,3rd,and 4th year students also participated there

Aaghaaz - annual fest

At the beggining of every year a cultural fest named "Aaghaaz" is organized by our seniors to tribute our passout 4th year students

College Sports

Every year our college organized many inter and intra college games like Football, Cricket, Volyball , Women Cricket and so on

Kartik Puja & Saraswati Puja

By the help of SWA (Student Welfare Association) every year we organise Kartik Puja at our college.
